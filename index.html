About Me

I'm a second-year Computer Science student at the University of Mauritius with a passion for technology. My journey with tech began early. Those A* grades in Computer Science at O and A Level weren't just academic achievements, they were signs that I'd found what I love.

During my recent traineeship at IBL Together as a Trainee Internal Auditor, I discovered the magic that happens when technology meets business strategy. I wasn't just working with Excel - I transformed raw audit data into compelling visual stories that actually influenced decision-making. That's when it clicked: technology isn't just about writing code, it's about creating an impact.

I believe that learning never stops. Everything I learn, from GUI development to PC Repair, isn't just a line in my resume - it's another tool in my problem-solving arsenal. But here's what sets me apart - I don't just learn for the sake of learning. I learn to build, to innovate and to make a difference.
